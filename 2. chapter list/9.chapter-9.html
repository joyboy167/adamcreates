Chapter 9 - The Second Showdown

SPECIAL CATCH: Catch 1 new 3-star pokemon with no catchrate limit (reccomend catching a starter)
(NOTE: try not to go below 45, cuz it will be hard with just pokeballs.)

(PREP FOR SHOWDOWN)

PREP:
- Your three pokemon from showdown I, need to be included in your team.
- Three more eligible pokemon need to be added to make a team of six.
- Update your roster draft before the showdown and send.
- Train your team to lv 22, and load your team into showdown.

LEVEL-CAP: 22
ROSTER: 11 POKEMON (1 NEW POKEMON)
STAGE & BST LIMIT: Any stage, BST 420 or lower.

FORMAT: 6 vs 6 battle (best of 5).
EVS: 30 EVs to spend per pokemon (DO RESEARCH OR SMOGON).
NATURES: CHOSE BEST NATURES (USE SMOGON OR RESEARCH)(CAN BE CHANGED IN POKECENTRE).
ABILITIES: CHOOSE IDEAL ABILITY IN SHOWDOWN (CAN PROJECT DESIRED ABILITY EVEN IF NOT IN GAME).
HELD ITEMS: ALL HOLDING POKEBALLS.
