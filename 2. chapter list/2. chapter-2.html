Pokemon Radical Red Walkthrough

Chapter 2 - Errands

- Do the "time changer" quiz with one of the lab assistants
*Ans: Pancham, 5 p.m, Never.

- Go to Viridrian Mart to get Oak's package and bring it back to him.

- Go to Gary's sister to get a map and then say bye to your mum.

- Go to Viridrian pokecentre to heal.

- Go to above Viridian to get the dexnav from a lab assistant.

- Go to the trainer school for the pokevial challenge.
*The building with a teacher teaching a student.

- Beat the teacher to obtain the PokeVial.

* HOW TO BEAT THE TEACHER:
Use skarmory, gible, and fletchling.
Skarmory sets up rocks then you switch to Gible.
Next, Gible dies and the next 5 Shedninjas go down.
Send out Fletchling and bravebird and now you won.
