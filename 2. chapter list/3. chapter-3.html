Pokemon Radical Red Walkthrough

Chapter 3 - First Pokemons and Rival Battles

ACCESS: Route 1, Routee 22 (Avoid Gary), and Viridian City Grass.
CATCHRATE: 120 OR HIGHER (CURRENT POKEBALLS ARE WEAK).
STAGE & BST LIMIT: FIRST STAGE ONLY, BST; 350 OR LOWER.

- Go to the pokecentre and heal to refill your pokevial.

- Go to the PokeMart, sell items, and buy pokeballs.

- Catch 1 pokemon from Route 1 (BST LIMIT).

- Catch 1 pokemon from Route 22 (BST LIMIT).

- Catch 1 pokemon from viridian grass (BST LIMIT).

- Head East to Route 22 to beat Gary.

- Head North to forest to beat Brandon for the exp.share.

ROSTER: 3 POKEMON (3 NEW + RELEASE YOUR PLACE HOLDER)
