Pokemon Radical Red Walkthrough

Chapter 3 - First Pokemon and Showdown

- Go to the pokecentre and heal to refill your pokevial.

- Go to the PokeMart, sell items, and buy pokeballs.

- (PREP FOR SHOWDOWN)

- ACCESS: Route 1, Route 22(avoid Gary), and Viridian city grass.

- POKEMOM: Catch 3 pokemon for your team and train them up.
*You can use the dexnav to register the pokemon you want to catch and find them.

- CATCH RATE: Try to stay above 120 as the pokeballs are weak.

- LEVEL CAP: 15

- BOSS BATTLE: SHOWDOWN
*Format: 3v3, lvl cap 15, 0/1 EVs.
